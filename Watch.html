Having a vastly different product from the V2 with a smaller design, one less input button and a colour screen listed the changes to the new watch that required the firmware to be rebuilt.

The aim was to ensure the watch have a more consistent feel whilst keeping all the functionality of the existing firmware.

Due to time constraints research was focused on feedback from the previous watch with iterative design feedback from internal stakeholders. This required co-ordination with the support team to ensure any issues from the V2 where not replicated in the new firmware.
