About The Company

NN4M is a software house that focuses on leading eCommerce application and has just recently been acquired by the Wunderman group. I worked here starting as a Junior UI/UX designer and be promoted to a mid-level.


My role

Within the company, I worked with a small team of designers that were dedicated to varying development teams depending on the client's workload at any given time.

In the year and a half I worked there I spent the majority of the time focussing my efforts on handling the Selfridges and Brown Thomas Clients alongside working with Elkjop and Listening into Action on smaller projects

A lot of my focus within the Junior portion was directly dealing with clients and ensuring the quality within both designs has high that would lead to Pixel Perfect designs. A few months on I took more responsibility with looking into clients user testing and how they produced it to creating concepts for new clients and partaking in presentations with hi-fidelity concepts to clients that shared user research they had created with us.


Results

As a result of working projects, we shipped numerous and successful new features for Selfridges and embedded a successful new process that works for Brown Thomas.
